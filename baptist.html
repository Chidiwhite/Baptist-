Welcome Message From the Pastor


Welcome to April, 2022, the month of Experiencing the Ressurection Power of Christ.
If Jesus was not raised from death, then all our preaching and
teaching of God's word is useless and our faith is useless.
Glory be to God that Jesus was truly raised from death.
We can tell the world about him; we can boast about Him, we can win all battles in him.
Oh! We are more than a conqueror.

Pastor Godwin Ofoegbu.
